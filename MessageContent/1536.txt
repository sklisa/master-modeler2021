 Juneteenth a portmanteau of June and nineteenth also known as Freedom Day Jubilee Day and Liberation Day started as a Texas state holiday but is now celebrated annually on the th of June throughout the United States to commemorate Union army general Gordon Granger announcing federal orders in the city of Galveston Texas on June proclaiming that all slaves in Texas were now free Although the Emancipation Proclamation had formally freed them almost two and a half years earlier and the American Civil War had largely ended with the defeat of the Confederate States in April Texas was the most remote of the slave states with a low presence of Union troops so enforcement of the proclamation had been slow and inconsistent Wikipedia
