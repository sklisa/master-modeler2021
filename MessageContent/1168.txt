On National Police Woman Day we honor Detective Kim Hernandez from San Bernardino Police Department Det Hernandez recently joined the ERASE Child Trafficking Team Det Hernandez is dedicated to making an impact on Human Trafficking Thank you Det Hernandez for your hard work Detective Hernandez has been a police officer with the San Bernardino Police Department since During her career she has been assigned to the Crime Impact Team Graffiti Task Force the Violent Crimes Task Force and the Vice Narcotics Unit In January she was assigned as the Vice Detective and began working cases involving the commercialized sex trade including Human Trafficking Pimping and Pandering Some of Detective Hernandez s accomplishments include participation in over operations targeting human trafficking more than criminal investigations arrests involving human trafficking felony cases filed years of state prison sentences Most importantly adults recovered and juveniles recovered from human trafficking situations In March she received the Meritorious Service Medal for her work in combating human trafficking In she was assigned to the San Bernardino County Human Trafficking Task Force which is managed by the San Bernardino Sheriff s Department and includes investigators from local state and federal agencies In May she was nominated for Officer of the Year by the San Bernardino City Chamber of Commerce for her continued work in Human Trafficking including successful prosecutions and training for the community and first responders Detective Hernandez instructs at the San Bernardino Sheriff s Department s Advanced Officer Training Center California POST and California Narcotic Officers Association In August Detective Hernandez was the recipient of The California Narcotic Officers Association Alfred E Stewart Award Region V for her distinguished and dedicated service in combating human trafficking
